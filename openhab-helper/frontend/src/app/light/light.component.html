Light Settings

Adjust the way how SmartBerry handles your lighting.

Light Switchable Items

Lights are triggered automatically in dependence to daylight and presence changes. Get started with a proper lights configuration by tagging your light items as light switchables.

SmartBerry will add manageable items to each location having light switchable items to let you adjust it's particular light configuration. Set the lighting mode in each location for dark, obscured and bright conditions. To do so, edit the generated members of gCore_Lights_DarkMode, gCore_Lights_ObscuredMode and gCore_Lights_BrightMode. Current choices are:

  • Turn lights off (OFF)
  • Turn lights on (ON)
  • Turn lights on presence (AUTO_ON)
  • Disable light changes (UNCHANGED)
  • Use lights to simulate presence (SIMULATE)

Light switchable items in locations with a lighting mode of AUTO_ON will turn off after a given time without presence events. Change Core_Lights_DefaultDuration to adjust the number of minutes until lights are turned off automatically.

Welcome light

If presence changes from AWAY_SHORT or AWAY_LONG to HOME, you can turn on all light switchable items in locations having the lighting mode set to AUTO_ON automatically. To do so, enable the corresponding welcome light item:

  • Core_Lights_WelcomeLight_DarkMode to enable welcome lights during dark light conditions.
  • Core_Lights_WelcomeLight_ObscuredMode to enable welcome lights during obscured light conditions.
  • Core_Lights_WelcomeLight_BrightMode to enable welcome lights during bright light conditions.

Light Measurement Items

SmartBerry analyzes light measurement items to identify the actual light conditions and change the state of Core_Lights_AmbientLightCondition accordingly. Note that light measurement items of locations with at least one active light switchable item are not put into consideration.

If no considerable light measurement items are available, SmartBerry can also use the sun state of an astro item to determine the actual light conditions.

Adjust the tresholds of Core_Lights_AmbientLightCondition_LuminanceTreshold_Obscured and Core_Lights_AmbientLightCondition_LuminanceTreshold_Dark to change the upper luminance level for obscured and dark light conditions.
